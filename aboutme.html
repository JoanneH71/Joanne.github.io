Who am I?

哈囉! I'm Joanne Hsieh, and I'm excited to share a bit about myself. I believe life is a grand adventure, and I'm here to make the most of every moment. Originally from Taiwan, I've had the privilege of experiencing different cultures and perspectives. Currently based in New Zealand, I've made it my mission to embrace new experiences and broaden my horizons. As a student, I'm passionate about art related subjects. I love diving deep into painting and designing, constantly seeking knowledge, and pushing the boundaries of what I can achieve. Outside of my professional pursuits, you'll often find me crocheting. When I have free time, you can always see me crocheting, either a flower or a stuffed animal. I believe in living a well-rounded life that brings joy and fulfilment. Through this platform, I aim to share my experiences, insights, and lessons learned along the way. Join me as we navigate this beautiful journey called life, inspire each other, and make lasting connections. I'm always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and meaningful conversations. Feel free to reach out, and let's embark on this adventure together!
