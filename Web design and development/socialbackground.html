Social Background

Born in the beautiful land of Taiwan, I embarked on a journey that would shape my perspective and open doors to new horizons. At the tender age of 5, I migrated with my family to the stunning shores of New Zealand. Growing up in these two vibrant cultures, I've been fortunate to embrace the best of both worlds. The rich heritage and traditions of Taiwan remain deeply rooted within me, while New Zealand has become my second home, with its warm and welcoming spirit. This multicultural upbringing has instilled in me a profound appreciation for diversity and the power of cross-cultural connections. It has fuelled my curiosity to explore different perspectives, languages, and ways of life. Through this platform, I aim to share my experiences as a bridge between these two remarkable countries. I invite you to join me in celebrating cultural fusion, breaking down barriers, and fostering a greater understanding of the world we live in.
