Interest/hobbies

I started crocheting when I was just eleven years old, in seventh grade. It was a new and exciting hobby for me. At first, I struggled with holding the crochet hook and working with the yarn. But I didn't give up. I practiced a lot and gradually got better. I remember the thrill of finishing my first scarf. It was a big accomplishment for me. Over the years, my love for crocheting has grown. I enjoy making more complicated projects now. Crocheting helps me relax and express my creativity. It's amazing to see how far I've come since those early days. I'm proud of my progress and excited to keep learning and making new things with my crochet skills.
